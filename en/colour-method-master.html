About
CTV?

In order to promote the effective use of CTV, The Hong Kong Printers Association (HKPA) has successfully applied funding from Cultural and Creative Industries Development Agency of the Government of the Hong Kong Special Administrative Region to organise the project “Colour. Method. Master : GBA ReCreates the World's Colour” ("Colour. Method. Master"), with Advanced Printing Technology Center (APTEC) as the implementation organisation.

CTV Pilot Scheme and Production of CTV Characterisation Dataset are two important schemes. APTEC has organized hybrid briefing session on Jun 11 and Jun 12, 2024, to introduce the contents of schemes. The response was very good. APTEC had received over 90 applications for both schemes. The vetting committee selected qualified printing companies on Jul 4, 2024.

In addition to the above two programmes, APTEC will also hold public training courses, participate in exhibitions, hold the "HK Summit - Global Printing Standardisation cum CTV Certificate Presentation Ceremony”,etc., comprehensively promote the "CTV Certified Printing Company", and promote different activities through different channels.
