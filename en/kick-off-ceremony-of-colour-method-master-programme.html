Kick-off Ceremony of “Colour. Method. Master : GBA ReCreates the World's Colour”cum Forum on the Development of New Quality Productive Forces in Printing

Advanced Printing Technology Centre (APTEC), under The Hong Kong Printers Association (HKPA) initiated the application of a printing colour control method - Colour Tone Value (CTV). It has been adopted as one of the first GBA printing organization standards. In order to promote the effective use of CTV, HKPA has successfully applied funding from Cultural and Creative Industries Development Agency to organise the project “Colour ꞏ Method ꞏ Master: GBA ReCreates the World's Colour”, with APTEC as the implementation organisation. The long-term goal of the HKPA and APTEC is to establish CTV technology as the mainstream method for color control in printing in China, and expand its adoption to the international printing industry, thereby solidifying China's position in the global printing sector.

  • Date: Sept 10, 2024 (Tue)
  • Guest of Honour: Mr. Victor Tsang, JP, Commissioner for Cultural and Creative Industries
  • Time: 2:30 - Kick-off ceremony | 3:00 - Forum
  • Venue: Grand Ballroom, Kowloon Shangri-la Hotel, 64 Mody Road, Tsim Sha Tsui East, Kowloon, Hong Kong
  • Fee: Free-of-charge
